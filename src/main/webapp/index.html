About US

Congun private limited (Construction Group United Network), our mission is to create a market place for construction equipment industry providing a Networking solution to an expensive machinery demand in India. We aspire to be the India’s largest equipment rental, buy/sell network, aiming to safely build a better and stronger future. We strive to be a competent network in the construction equipment industry with continuous improvement to achieve benchmark reliability and sustainability.

Our Services

Find required equipment

Post equipment requirement for your project/need. Get wide range of suppliers matching your requirement with competitive quotes. Choose the best supplier and negotiate.

Rent your equipment

Increase your business by renting equipment to a larger verified client/customer, generate more money and reduce idle time. Quote the price to preferred client and location & get hired faster.

Buy or Sell Used Equipment

Users can buy/sell their used equipment through our portal. Reduce the cost of marketing without the need to place ads or a third party for buying/selling the equipment.
