What we do?

Congun is a heavy equipment rental market place, connecting clients and suppliers from all over India. Through our platform, users can hire/rent equipments at their desired locations and at economical prices. We serve a broad range of end-users, including construction companies, equipment rental companies, mining and irrigation, pipeline infrastructure & telecommunication, industrial plants, highway projects, real estate, located in major cities in India.

Congun has always striven to serve heavy equipment clients/buyers and suppliers/sellers in the most comprehensive and convenient way possible. We provide a market place where users can find, compare, communicate and hire/rent high quality equipments and buy/sell used equipments.
