Frequently Asked Questions


Who is a client?

A person or a company who is looking to hire or buy equipment through Congun.


Who is a supplier?

A person or a company who is looking to rent or sell equipment through Congun.


How do I register as a client?

To register as a Client:
Log on to www.congun.com.
Click Sign up
Select Client, enter the required details and click Submit


How do I register as a supplier?

To register as a Supplier:
Log on to www.congun.com.
Click Sign up
Select Supplier, enter the required details and click Submit


What are the benefits of the client?

  • Opportunity to post your requirement, source the equipment, and explore the broad range of matching equipment
  • Choose the best match from the verified suppliers and negotiate (interact) with them directly
  • Fully transparent and easy process to help you make quick decisions


What are the benefits of the Supplier?

  • Large base of verified clients to rent or sell your equipment to
  • More requirements that match your equipment, reduce idle time, and generate more revenue
  • Fully transparent and easy process to help you make quick decisions


How do I post a requirement for equipment and receive quotes from suppliers?

Sign up with Congun as a client
Log in to your account
Click Rental board
Click Post a requirement
Enter the details of the equipment you are looking for and click Submit
Your requirement is now displayed to Suppliers. You will receive quotes submitted by Suppliers


How do I see quotes submitted by Suppliers for my requirement?

Log in to your account
Click Rental board, under Quotes, click View
Quotes table displays. Quotes by all Suppliers for your requirement are listed here. You can shortlist, hire, or reject quotes. Click Shortlist to see the contact details of the Supplier Click Hire to hire the equipment from the Supplier Click Reject to reject the quote from the Supplier


How do I post the details of equipment I want to rent to the clients?

Sign up with Congun as a Supplier
Log in to your account
Click Add new equipment
Enter the details of the equipment in Add Equipment Form, and click Submit Use Option 1 to choose from the list of popular equipment Use Option 2 if your equipment is not in the list.


How do I see recommendations matching my equipment and submit quotes?

When clients post their requirements which match your equipment, you will receive recommendations.
Log in to your account
Click Rental board, and check for matching requirements under Recommendation for you
Click Submit a Quote, enter the details in Quote Form, and click Submit Client will receive your quote and contact you if they are interested in your equipment.

How do I add my equipment to sell?

Log in to your account
Click Used equipment
Click Sell my equipment
Click Add equipment to sell
Enter the details of the equipment in Add Equipment to Sell Form and click Submit


How do I know who has shown interest to buy my equipment?

Log in to your account
Click Used equipment
Under My equipment for sale, click the number listed as Interested People for your equipment
Details of interested people are displayed. Contact them, negotiate, and finalize your deal.


How do I see the equipment for sale and buy it?

Log in to your account
Click Used equipment
Click Equipment for sale
All the equipment for sale is displayed here.
You can filter the equipment by category, manufacture, and location.
Click Interest to view the details of the Seller
Contact the Seller, negotiate, and finalize the deal.


Do I pick up my equipment or is it delivered?

You can discuss the terms and conditions of the equipment delivery with the supplier and make a suitable decision.


How do I verify the equipment documentation?

You can ask the supplier to provide the documentation of the equipment and verify it before you finalize the deal.


How do I receive payment from clients?

You can receive payment through cheques, drafts, or bank transfers.


Can I register as both client and supplier with Congun?

No. You can either register as a client or a supplier.
